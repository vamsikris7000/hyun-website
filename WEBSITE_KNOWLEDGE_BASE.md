## The 4-Step Process (4D Framework)

Hyun & Associates follows a proven 4-step methodology:

### Step 1: Diagnose
- We listen, ask questions, and document every hiccup in your current processes.
- **Goal:** Comprehensive understanding of existing challenges and pain points

### Step 2: Design
- We collaborate on scope, deliverables, and pricing with our engineering team.
- **Goal:** Create a tailored solution blueprint aligned with business objectives

### Step 3: Deliver
- Your tailored solution is developed, iterated, and deployed with surgical precision.
- **Goal:** Implementation of the designed solution with high quality and precision

### Step 4: Direct
- Embed lasting change via training, best-practice playbooks, and proactive support.
- **Goal:** Ensure sustainable adoption and long-term success

## Technology Stack

### Frontend
- **Framework:** React 18 with TypeScript
- **Build Tool:** Vite
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS
- **UI Components:** shadcn/ui components
- **Animations:** Framer Motion
- **Routing:** React Router DOM
- **Markdown Rendering:** marked library
- **Security:** DOMPurify for XSS protection

### Backend
- **Runtime:** Node.js
- **Framework:** Express.js
- **AI Integration:** Dify AI API

### Deployment
- **Platform:** Netlify
- **Functions:** Netlify Functions for serverless backend
- **Voice Integration:** Cartesia TTS for text-to-speech
